Frequently Asked Questions


I don't have an email address. How do I get one?

Need an email address? No problem! There are many free, web-based, e-mail providers that are currently available. It usually takes just a few minutes to create an account, too. If you go to www.gmail.com you can click on the link that says "Create an Account." If you go to www.hotmail.com, you can click the link that says "Sign up." There is also www.yahoo.com, where you can click the link that says "Sign up." Then you can pick an email address and password and enter some minimal personal information to create your very own email account. You can then log in and access your email from any computer with internet access. These are just a few of the free email account providers that are currently available. You may be able to find another one that suits your needs even better.

What browsers and computing platforms work with Hourly?

Hourly supports the following browsers on a Windows-based PC or an Apple Macintosh: Administrators and Team Members: Internet Explorer 9, 10 and 11 Mozilla Firefox (latest release) Google Chrome (latest release) Safari (latest release) Job Application: Internet Explorer 9, 10 and 11 Mozilla Firefox (latest release) Google Chrome (latest release) Safari (latest release) Administrative access and use of Hourly is not supported on the iPad or any other tablet or Smart Phone. Internet Explorer 6, Opera, and mobile browsers are not supported.
